    About Austria

    The German-speaking country of Austria is not bypassed by many travelers. Austria can offer a varied program for the study of classical European architecture, an abundance of classical music for its connoisseurs, the purest mineral waters on a spa vacation, as well as professional high-quality tracks in the ski resorts of the Alps. Despite high prices, Austrian ski resorts remain competitive thanks to well-equipped pistes, comfortable hotels and well-groomed slopes. In addition, every corner of the country is remembered for the fact that one or another famous person lived there in the past - Mozart, Strauss, Beethoven, Wolfgang or anyone else. World famous Vienna boasts huge art galleries, classical palaces and park ensembles, theaters, opera halls with waltzes and charming Viennese cafes with sweets. The picturesque Vienna Woods, which includes a number of Vienna's outskirts, has become a special object for tourists to visit. To visit the historical homeland of many musical and literary celebrities, you need to take care of obtaining an Austrian visa or any other Schengen visa. A visa to Austria can be opened at the German consulate. The procedure for applying for a visa to Austria is the same as for German visas. It is necessary to pay special attention to the correctness of the translated documents into German or English, so that due to incorrect translations there is no refusal to issue an Austrian visa. To obtain a Schengen visa to Austria, your passport must have at least two Schengen visas over the past two years, or you need to prove financial viability for the entire period of the trip. It is even more difficult to obtain an annual visa. A transit visa to Austria is no longer in demand. To apply for a business (business, work) visa to Austria, you need to prepare a special package of documents for obtaining an EU blue card. The package must certainly contain an offer from the employer with an indication of wages and the amount of working time. Holders of an Austrian visa can take a direct flight from Minsk to Vienna. If you want to visit Austria, you can apply for a Schengen visa with us!
